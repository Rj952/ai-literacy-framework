# AI Literacy Framework Platform

**Created by Rohan Jowallah**

A comprehensive AI literacy framework platform aligned with the U.S. Department of Labor standards.

## U.S. Policy Alignment

- U.S. Department of Labor AI Literacy Framework (2025)
- - Executive Order 14110
  - - National AI Initiative Act (2020)
    - - NIST AI Risk Management Framework
      - - Blueprint for an AI Bill of Rights
        - - WIOA
          - - NAIRR Task Force Report
            - - Department of Education AI Guidance
